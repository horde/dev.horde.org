_getChart (line 129)

Get a stock chart. Types available:

Intraday - b = intraday/big; t = intraday/small; w = week/big; v = week/small ex: $_intraDayChartUrl . '/b?s=aapl'

Normal charts - 0b = year/small; 3m = 3month/big; 1y = 1yr/big; 2y = 2yr/big; 5y = 5yr/big; add s to chart against S&P500 add m to include moving average ex: $_chartUrl . '0b/a/aapl.png'

Dimensions are 512,288 for big charts and 192,96 for small ones.
