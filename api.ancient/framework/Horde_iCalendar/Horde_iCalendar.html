Whether entry is vcalendar 1.0, vcard 2.1 or vnote 1.1.


These 'old' formats are defined by www.imc.org. The 'new' (non-old)  formats icalendar 2.0 and vcard 3.0 are defined in rfc2426 and rfc2445  respectively.
